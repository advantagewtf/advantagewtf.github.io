Spotify Integration Guide

This guide will walk you through the process of setting up Spotify integration with Advantage, allowing you to control your music directly from the game.

Prerequisites

  • A Spotify account
  • Latest version of Advantage (Spotify branch) installed
  • Internet connection

Step-by-Step Setup Guide

1

Create a Spotify Developer Account

Visit the Spotify Developer Portal and sign in with your Spotify account credentials.

If this is your first time using the Spotify Developer Portal, you'll need to agree to the terms and conditions.

2

Create a New Application

Once logged in:

  1. Click on the "Dashboard" link in the top menu
  2. Select "Create an App"
  3. Enter a name for your application (e.g., "Advantage")
  4. Add a brief description (e.g., "Spotify integration for Advantage")
  5. Check the agreement box and click "Create"
3

Configure Your Application

After creating your app, you'll need to configure it:

  1. In your app's dashboard, click on "Edit Settings"
  2. Under "Redirect URIs", add: http://127.0.0.1:8888/callback
  3. Save the changes

Note: The redirect URI must match exactly what's configured in Advantage. If you're experiencing connection issues, double-check that this URI is entered correctly.

4

Get Your API Credentials

On your app's dashboard, you'll find two important pieces of information:

  1. Client ID - Visible on the main app page
  2. Client Secret - Click "Show Client Secret" to reveal

Important: Keep your Client Secret secure! Never share it publicly or commit it to public repositories.

5

Configure Advantage

Now, open Advantage and follow these steps:

  1. Open the Advantage menu
  2. Find the "Music" section
  3. Enter your Client ID in the designated field
  4. Enter your Client Secret in the designated field
  5. Click "Save" or "Next"
  6. Click "Connect with Spotify"
6

Authorize the Connection

A browser window will open asking you to authorize Advantage to access your Spotify account:

  1. Log in to your Spotify account if prompted
  2. Review the permissions that Advantage is requesting
  3. Click "Agree" or "Authorize"

After authorization, you'll be redirected back to Advantage, and the connection will be established.

7

Testing the Connection

To ensure that everything is working properly:

  1. Open Spotify and start playing a song

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

  • Connection Failed: Verify that your Client ID and Client Secret are entered correctly.
  • Authorization Error: Make sure you've added the correct redirect URI in your Spotify app settings.
